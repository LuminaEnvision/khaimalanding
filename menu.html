Food should nourish the body without distracting from the experience.

Rooted in Mediterranean and Middle Eastern traditions, our cuisine is generous, plant-forward, and designed for sharing.

As retreat culture grew on the island, so did the need for reliable, conscious catering. Khaima Retreat Catering was born from that need bringing restaurant-quality food into retreat spaces with professionalism and care.
