Our Story

Born
from a
simple
idea.

Food should bring people together in a way that feels grounding, generous, and alive.

Not heavy. Not complicated. Not rushed.
Just food that feels honest and nourishing.

On Koh Phangan, we found a community that gathers to move, to breathe, to heal, and to reconnect. We saw that food plays a quiet but essential role in that journey.

Inspired by Mediterranean and Middle Eastern traditions, Khaima brings together fresh vegetables, legumes, herbs, olive oil, spices, and citrus — flavors rooted in sharing and simplicity.

What began as a restaurant evolved naturally into retreat catering. Retreat leaders asked for the same warmth and balance they experienced at our table — brought into their retreat spaces.

So we built a system that supports the rhythm of retreats:

Light breakfasts. Balanced lunches. Grounding dinners.
Always vegetarian-forward. Always adaptable. Always prepared with care.
Khaima is about supporting the space you are holding.

When you host a retreat, your focus should be on your people.

Not heavy. Not complicated. Not rushed. Just food that feels honest and nourishing.

We take care of the rest.
